    > "Help! I am Rahul. A man with a knife is threatening people in the basement parking area of the mall. He is blocking the exit and shouting aggressively."

4.  **Domestic Violence (Critical):**
    > "I am Anita. My husband is beating me at our home in Block B. He has a weapon and has locked the door. Please send help immediately."

5.  **Discreet SOS ("Angel Shot" Mode):**
